Hello!

I'm a Vancouver-based UI/UX designer who started out in fashion.

That transition from clothes to code at BCIT taught me something important: whether you're wearing an outfit or using an app, it has to feel right.

My background in fashion gives me a different eye for visual storytelling, but I've paired that with the technical side of product design to make sure what I build actually works for people. I don't just want to make things look "stunning" -I want to make sure they feel effortless to use.

I'm the kind of designer who spends as much time listening to users as I do sketching out new flows. For me, a high-quality digital experience should be fun and engaging, but it also needs to be kind to the user. I'm always looking for ways to push creative boundaries while keeping the human on the other side of the screen in mind.

I'm a big believer in collaboration and staying open to new ideas, so I'm always down to chat about new opportunities. Let's build something cool together!
